Information Gathering

We use web crawler to gather all the information precisely. The information is from websites like http://mypage.zju.edu.cn/ and http://bsdt.zju.edu.cn, two of public websites of Zhejiang University.

Mobile Navigation

Amap is a free api to be used to look for your destination. When you want to go somewhere, you can search it in our app, and you will get what you want immediately!
